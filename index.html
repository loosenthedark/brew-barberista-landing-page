Supporting local business

We are an independent coffee shop and we have chosen to work with quality and, where possible, local producers.

Our coffee is from 3fe, who are based in nearby Glasnevin. They have been in business since 2011, and have been roasting their own coffee since 2014. All of their coffee is sourced from some of the best farms and co-ops around the world, where they have built up long-standing relationship with the producers.

Our delicious artisan pastries are supplied by Tartine Organic Bakery, which is located just down the road from us in Baldoyle.
